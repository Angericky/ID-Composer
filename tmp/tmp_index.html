Key Features

1

Interleaved Multimodal Chain-of-Thought (iMCoT)

Closed-loop reasoning that validates steps against visual feedback to minimize hallucinations.

2

Synergistic Editor-Evaluator Optimization (SEPO)

Dual-loop system where the model acts as both editor and evaluator, refining strategies via intrinsic rewards.

3

Unified Preservative & Generative Editing

Seamlessly integrates Adobe Lightroom (200+ tools) and Qwen-Image-Edit for the full spectrum of editing tasks.

4

Self-Reflective Learning Mechanism

Automatically generates reflection trajectories to learn from mistakes and optimize tool selection.

Data Distribution

ArtEdit Dataset—a bilingual dataset (English & Chinese) with 230K samples.

1

Rich Task Taxonomy

30 task types balanced between 15 preservative (color grading, exposure) and 15 generative (object removal, style transfer, background replacement) editing tasks.

2

Wide Scenario Coverage

10 major scene categories with 37+ subcategories covering portraits, landscapes, architecture, wildlife, urban scenes, still life, and design graphics.

3

Structured Training Subsets

ArtEdit-Lr (120K) for retouching, ArtEdit-Gen (60K) for generation, and ArtEdit-Eval (50K) for evaluation.
